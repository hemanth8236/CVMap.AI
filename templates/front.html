Welcome to CVMap.AI

      CVMap.AI is an innovative AI-powered platform designed to provide the comprehensive support 
      to students across all facets of crafting an impressive CV or resume. It boasts an array of 
      remarkable capabilities, including question prediction to anticipate interview inquiries,
      accurate job post prediction, and even automated resume generation. With CVMap.Ai, students
      can harness the power of cutting-edge technology to enhance their resume-building process,
      ensuring that their qualifications, experiences, and skills are effectively showcased. By
      leveraging AI-driven insights, students can gain a competitive edge in the job market, a
      creating a compelling narrative that resonates with potential employers. Whether it's the
      crafting personalized responses, foreseeing job requirements, or streamlining the resume
      creation journey, CVMap.AI is poised to revolutionize how students approach and succeed
      in their career aspirations.

CVScore.AI

          CVScore is a web application designed to automatically analyze and score 
          resumes (CVs) based on their content, format, and relevance to specific 
          job roles. It leverages natural language processing and the rule-based 
          extraction techniques to evaluate the quality and completeness of key 
          resume sections, such as experience, education, skills, projects, 
          achievements, and professional profiles like LinkedIn and GitHub.
